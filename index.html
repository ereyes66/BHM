Ashton Sanders

Bio

Ashton Sanders is an accomplished actor known for his captivating performances on both stage and screen. Born in California, he embarked on his acting journey at a young age, honing his craft through dedication and perseverance. With breakout roles in critically acclaimed films such as "Moonlight" and "The Equalizer 2," Sanders has proven himself as a versatile talent with a knack for bringing depth and authenticity to his characters. Continuously pushing boundaries, he continues to captivate audiences with his compelling portrayals and remains a rising star in the entertainment industry.

2018 Winner of CinEuphoria Award, Someone to Watch - Honorary Award

Interesting Facts

  • Ashton Sanders was originally discovered by a talent agent while performing in a high school production.
  • He earned widespread acclaim for his role as teenage Chiron in the Oscar-winning film "Moonlight."
  • Sanders is known for his dedication to his craft, often immersing himself deeply in the characters he portrays.
  • Outside of acting, he is passionate about social justice issues and has used his platform to advocate for change, particularly around racial equality and representation in Hollywood.
